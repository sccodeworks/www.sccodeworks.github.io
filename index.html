A Warehouse Management System

Developed For Operations, By Operations

SC Codeworks is an all-inclusive software development company focused on the warehouse and transportation industries. Founded over 20 years ago, our seasoned professionals provide a state-of-the-art, scalable, and customizable Warehouse Management System (WMS).

  • Our clients see us as an extension of their team, thanks to our comprehensive warehouse management expertise and a WMS that continuously evolves to meet customer needs and industry requirements.
  • From large-scale manufacturers, 3PL providers, and distribution centers, we help our customers grow. Our on-site Operations Consulting helps improve warehouse efficiency from day one. Most of our programmers and support staff come from warehouse logistics backgrounds, enhancing our ability to bring meaningful improvements to life.
  • SC Codeworks is a fully robust WMS that can satisfy all of your warehousing needs and is designed to your custom work processes rather than the other way around
  • As real partners with our customers, we have grown to offer operations systems consulting, software installation, cloud hosting, programming development, and support, along with EDI communications, systems integrations, and training
  • Our goal is to help warehouse and logistics operations like yours thrive every day, offering sophisticated functionality with a simplified approach youll find refreshing.
  • SC Codeworks supports multiple companies with diverse requirements and inventory to reduce costs, improve performance, and increase sales.
